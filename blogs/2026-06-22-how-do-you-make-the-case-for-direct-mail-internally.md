A framework for making the internal business case for direct mail across three scenarios: launching, defending, or growing a program. When launching, lead with consumer engagement stats such as 84% of consumers reading direct mail the same day they receive it; when defending, focus on proof of performance and the cost of cutting; when expanding, identify untapped opportunity in underserved customer segments.
