USPS offers limited-time promotional programs that provide postage discounts ranging from 3-7% for mailers meeting specific creative or technical requirements. Key promotions include Tactile/Sensory Interactive mail, Integrated Technology (including QR codes and AR), and First-Class Mail Advertising, with the biggest opportunity coming from stacking multiple promotions together for greater savings. Success requires advance registration and careful planning before campaign launch.
