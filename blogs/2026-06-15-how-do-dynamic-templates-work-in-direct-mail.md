Dynamic templates combine fixed design elements with variable placeholders that populate with recipient-specific data like names, addresses, and offer codes during production, with conditional logic to show or hide sections. Lob cites its State of Direct Mail Consumer Insights report finding that 67% of consumers say a personalized element prompted them to take action, and notes templates are only as good as the data behind them.
