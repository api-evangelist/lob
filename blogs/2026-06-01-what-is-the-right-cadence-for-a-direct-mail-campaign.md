The appropriate mailing frequency depends on campaign type and objectives, with promotional campaigns benefiting from a two-touch approach - an initial piece to introduce the offer and a follow-up reminder before it expires. Operational communications like statements require consistent, predictable timing tied to key dates. When integrating mail into multichannel strategies, coordinating timing across channels ensures cohesive messaging and maximum impact.
